Tokyo Is Gorgeous!

Close

Tokyo is the capital and the largest city of Japan. The city is super busy, and yet very beautiful and clean. The city is full of tall skyscrapers and the people in Tokyo are always in hustle and bustle. The people speak Japanese and they don't speak English very well. When you walk on the street, you always feel like you walk too slow. The transportation system is exceptionally advanced and the trains can bring you to almost every corner of the city.The Japanese food is good and the people there are nice. Tokyo is very fun to visit and you don't want to miss it!

Taipei Is Awesome!

Close

Taipei is the capital of Taiwan. It is the cultural, political and economical center of the country. The city also has a lot of tall commercial buildings and the people in Taipei are also very nice.They speak mandarin and don't speak English much. Taipei also has a very advanced transportation system and you can easily find the bus and train stations. Taipei is also famous for its night markets and these night markets are full of authentic Taiwanese street foods. The food is yummy and the people are great. Both tourists and local residents love to eat in the night markets. Taipei is a great city!

Seoul Is Wonderful!

Close

Seoul is the capital of South Korea. It is the political and economical hub of the country. The city is full of high skyscrapers and traditional Korean houses. Just like Tokyo and Taipei, it has an extremely dense and advanced transportation system. You can easily to everywhere with the train. There are a variety choice of food in Seoul, and I love Korean kimchee! The people in Korea speak Korean, and they don't underand English very much. The food are very special and the people are helpful. Seoul is a very unique city and you will like it too. I hope I will go there again soon!

About Ka Man's Travel

We LOVE Travel!

My husband and I love to bring our kids to places. We want them to go see the big world, so they know that there are many people living in many different places. We want them to realize and respect other people by going to countries and experiencing the distinct cultures.

In the last few years, we went to some Asian cities including Tokyo in Japan, Taipei in Taiwan, and Seoul in Korea. People in each of these countries speak different languages, have special living habits, believe in different religions, etc. We visited different places and we want the kids to understand that there is no better or worse; they are just different.

In this website, I want to show you the countries that we visited. All of these cities have the extremely convenient transporation systems, and tourists like us can go visit many sightseeing points by taking buses, taxis and trains. The city plannings are very unique; they are full of skyscrapers as well as the traditional architectures. Besides, the food in these places are very yummy too. We want to show you what we saw, what we ate, and what we came across.
